FAQ

Why do you need to know my marginal tax rate?

HSA contributions are tax-deductible, meaning that you can subtract them from your taxable income. For example, if you have a 25% tax rate and contribute $5,000 of your money to an HSA, you'll pay $1,250 less in taxes this year. Win!
